Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Site Auditor respects your privacy and is dedicated to maintaining high standards of data security and transparency. Because this is a utility auditing system, our data collection practices are minimal and fully focused on transparency.

1. Information We Process

When you submit a URL for analysis, the Site Auditor engine performs standard network lookups to query and verify HTTP configuration states. Specifically:

  • Scanned URLs: We process target URLs in real-time to retrieve open-source HTML structure and configuration headers. This data is handled in memory and is not written to permanent databases.
  • No Personal Harvesting: We do not ask for or collect names, email addresses, payment profiles, or accounts to run site audits.

2. Cookies & Client Tracking

This application does not make use of third-party tracking, analytical, or advertising cookies on your system. While our engine inspects cookie security parameters on the targets you audit, we do not deploy trackers on you.

3. External Connections

To run scans, our backend makes outgoing connections directly to the domains specified in the search forms. We operate independently of external search engines. Outgoing socket queries are conducted securely.

4. Contact Details

For inquiries regarding privacy settings or scan results processing, contact Arpit Ramesan directly via arpitramesan777@gmail.com.
